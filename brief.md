### Product at Launch

**The Tarrant — Origin** (the first product in the range)

Hand-stitched leather carry organiser. Not a bag, not a case — it exists in its own category. Slim, deliberate, and precisely engineered. Holds every essential a modern professional carries, and turns over to become a flat working surface.

**3 colourways:**

1. **Midnight** — Deep rich black, silver hardware, contrast white stitching
2. **Slate** — Complex cool charcoal, subtle and architectural
3. **Natural** — Raw pale leather, gold hardware, extraordinary visible grain

**Key features:**

- Full-grain leather construction, hand-stitched with contrast thread detail on darker colourways
- Moulded internal pocket structure — precision-engineered carry system
- D-ring carry hardware with strap
- Turns over to become a flat working surface
- Slim, architectural profile
- Holds: laptop, stylus/pen, iPhone, AirPods, charger, keys, glasses

**Price point:** $450–$600 AUD (mid-range premium positioning)

**Use cases:** Daily commute, business trips, laptop and work essentials, design on the run, client meetings, work on the go, onsite design consultations

## Section 05 — Design & Visual Style

### Colour Palette — A/B Options

Two palette directions to present to the client. Same colour family (warm, leather-inspired tones) but inverted visual weight.

#### Palette A — Dark-Led (from brand document)

Dark backgrounds dominate. Product and text float on near-black. Gold accent for CTAs and details.

| Role | Colour | Hex | Usage |
|------|--------|-----|-------|
| Primary | Near-black | `#0F0F12` | Hero sections, main backgrounds |
| Primary | Deep charcoal | `#1A1A1F` | Secondary backgrounds, cards |
| Accent | Gold | `#C4A072` | CTAs, links, highlights |
| Supporting | Warm off-white | `#E8E4DE` | Body text, story sections |
| Supporting | Natural leather tone | `#E8E0D4` | Alternate light sections |

**Feel:** Editorial, luxury, gallery-like. Product commands attention against dark space.

#### Palette B — Light-Led (from palette images)

Uses the 60/30/10 rule. Light, airy, architectural. Drawn from Parisian stone, atelier leather, and deep umber.

**60% Dominant — Cream & Linen / Ivory & Stone** (backgrounds, hero sections, breathing room)

| Colour | Hex | Usage |
|--------|-----|-------|
| Cream | `#F2EDE5` | Primary backgrounds, hero sections |
| Linen | `#EAE4D9` | Alternate backgrounds, cards |
| Ivory | `#E8E2D6` | Content sections |
| Stone | `#D5CEC3` | Borders, dividers, subtle structure |

**30% Secondary — Sand & Cognac / Umber & Wheat** (structure, warmth, mid-tones)

| Colour | Hex | Usage |
|--------|-----|-------|
| Wheat | `#C8AD8A` | Subheadings, secondary elements |
| Sand | `#B49672` | Navigation, labels |
| Cognac | `#9B7650` | CTAs, buttons, links |
| Umber | `#7D5C3A` | Hover states, active elements |

**10% Accent — Espresso & Obsidian** (focus, contrast, authority)

| Colour | Hex | Usage |
|--------|-----|-------|
| Espresso | `#2E1F14` | Headings, primary text |
| Obsidian | `#1C1714` | Logo, hero text, bold accents |

**Feel:** Warm, considered, architectural. Light space with rich leather tones and quiet dark authority.

- Generic bag/accessory brand aesthetics
- Anything that looks like a generic bag or fashion brand
