Our Story

The name Tarrant
carries lineage.

Everything here is chosen carefully, because everything here is.

Click to comment

It started with a
stone in her hand.

As a child, she was deeply tactile — often found rolling a stone in her hand, making umbrellas out of ferns, pressing bark into sheets not yet knowing why — only that it had to be tried.

That bark did not make fabric. But it made the base for paint. Failure, it turned out, was just the beginning of something else.

Click to comment

Self-taught.
Material-led.

Self-taught across painting, moulding and leatherwork, everything made under the Tarrant name starts the same way: with curiosity about what a material could become in the hands.
